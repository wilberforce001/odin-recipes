Grilled Basil Chicken Recipe

A very simple marinade of olive oil and chopped, fresh basil is all you need to season this grilled chicken dinner. Fresh and flavorful served with grilled veggies make a very healthy and light outdoor Summer Meal.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 teaspooncoarse ground black pepper
  • 4 skinned chicken breast halves
  • 1/4 cup freshly chopped basil leaves
  • 2 TBS olive oil
  • 2 TBS shredded Romano or Parmesan cheese, divided

Steps:

  1. Prepare grill to med/high heat.
  2. Rub the coarsely ground pepper into the meaty side of the chicken breast halves.
  3. In a small bowl, stir the chopped basil into the olive oil.
  4. Brush the chicken lightly with this mixture.
  5. Sit chicken in refrigerator for at least 20 minutes to marinate and infuse flavors.
  6. Grill the chicken over med/high heat for 8 to 10 minutes on each side, basting basting with any remaining marinade.
  7. Garnish chicken with a sprinkle of Romano or Parmesan cheese if desired.
  8. Serve the grilled chicken with grilled, in season vegetables.

Makes 4 servings
